AL Cigna: Max in-network rate for 97153

According to Cigna in AL, the most common rate for a 15-minute unit of 97153 is $85. The minimum rate is $15.3, while the maximum rate is $85. Some states have a significant variance of rates, while others may only have 1 or 2 standard rates — this often reflects payer-specific reimbursement policies, for which some only have a standard "rack rate" while others may negotiate with providers on a case by case basis. In addition, some payers have "tiers" of reimbursement that depend on provider factors such as how long the provider has been in service, relative volume of clients, and service adequacy in that region.
